Pasta Salad

Description

Loaded and refreshing Italian Pasta Salad! Made with tender pasta, smoked salami, fresh veggies and herbs, two types of cheese, and a tangy homemade Italian dressing.

Ingredients

  • pasta noodles of your choice
  • olive oil
  • red wine vinegar
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • salami
  • black olives
  • red onion
  • cherry tomatoes
  • fresh parsley
  • parmesan

Steps

  1. Make Dressing by combining olive oil, red wine vinegar, parsley, onion, lemon juice, basil, oregano, salt, sugar, and pepper.
  2. Cook Pasta according to package instructions. Drain water and rinse pasta in cold water. Allow to cool, then pour half of the salad dressing and toss to combine.
  3. Add Ingredients: Add mozzarella cheese pearls, salami, black olives, red onion, cherry tomatoes, fresh parsley, parmesan, pepperoncini (optional), and additional dressing, to taste. Refrigerate pasta salad with Italian dressing for 1 hour or longer, before serving.
