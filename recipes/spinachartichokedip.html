Spinach Artichoke Dip

Description

This ultra-creamy Spinach and Artichoke Dip tastes just like the Applebee's version! It's easy to make at home in under 30 minutes, just combine the ingredients until melted! This is perfect with tortilla chips, pita chips, crusty Artisan breads, and vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. roasted garlic alfredo sauce
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup Romano cheese
  • 2 cups low-moisture whole milk shredded mozzerella cheese
  • 28 oz artichoke hearts
  • 16 oz fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Add the alfredo sauce to a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Cut the cream cheese into cubes and mix it into the alfredo sauce until well-combined and creamy. Use a fork to whisk any lumps out.
  3. Add the Parmesan, Romano, and Mozzarella cheeses and stir until well-combined.
  4. Stir in the artichoke hearts, then add the spinach. Stir until well combined
  5. If the dip is thicker than desired, add up to a Â½ cup of milk and stir until combined and heated through.
  6. Serve with tortilla chips, pita chips, fresh bread, and/or vegetables.
